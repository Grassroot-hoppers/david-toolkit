# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project exists to build useful, open tools for independent businesses. We want a contribution culture that is demanding, clear, and welcoming.

Examples of behavior that contributes to that:

- critique the work, not the person
- show your reasoning
- document sharp disagreements honestly
- keep operator reality in view

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- condescension toward non-technical contributors
- vague or performative gatekeeping
- publishing private business data without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors whose behavior harms the project.

For conduct concerns, contact: `staycreative@julien.care`

This document is adapted from the Contributor Covenant spirit, simplified for this repo.
